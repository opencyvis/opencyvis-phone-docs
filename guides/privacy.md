# Privacy & Security

An AI agent with full phone access is one of the most privileged pieces of software you can run. This is not a place for "trust us."

## You Choose the AI Service

OpenCyvis is model-agnostic. Use a cloud provider, a private API, or a local model. Your data goes where you point it.

## No Telemetry

Zero tracking code. No analytics, no crash reporters, no phone-home. The app does not contact any server unless you configure an LLM provider.

## Open Source

Every line is public. Anyone can audit the code, verify what data is collected (none), and confirm what permissions are used and why.

## Local Model Privacy

With Ollama, the LLM runs on your hardware. No API keys, no network requests. Nothing leaves your device — not even the screen contents shown to the model.
