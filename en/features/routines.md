# Routines

Save frequent operations and run them on a schedule or with one tap.

## Examples

- **"Check calendar, weather, and unread emails every morning at 8am"** — the AI runs automatically and pushes a summary to chat.
- **"Clock in when I arrive at the office"** — geofence triggers the AI to open your attendance app and tap the button.
- **"Mute phone and set alarm for 7am"** — one tap every night.

## How It Works

1. Give the AI a task naturally — e.g., "check my schedule tomorrow"
2. After the AI completes it, tap **Save as Routine**
3. Choose a trigger: **scheduled time** or **location geofence**
4. The routine runs automatically, or tap it from the home screen to run manually.
